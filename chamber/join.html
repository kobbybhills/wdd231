NP Membership Benefits

This membership is free and designed for non-profit organizations. Benefits include:

  • Basic listing in the Chamber Directory.
  • Access to the monthly Chamber newsletter.
  • Invitation to general member meetings and networking events.

Bronze Membership Benefits

Perfect for start-ups and small businesses. Benefits include:

  • All NP Membership benefits.
  • Enhanced listing in the Chamber Directory.
  • Discounts on selected Chamber events and workshops.
  • Opportunity to host one networking event per year.

Silver Membership Benefits

Designed for growing businesses looking for more exposure. Benefits include:

  • All Bronze Membership benefits.
  • Priority placement in the Chamber Directory.
  • Opportunities for speaking engagements at Chamber events.
  • One complimentary advertisement in the monthly newsletter.
  • Exclusive access to advanced business training.

Gold Membership Benefits

Our premium package for established businesses seeking maximum impact. Benefits include:

  • All Silver Membership benefits.
  • Spotlight feature on the Chamber homepage for one month per year.
  • Unlimited complimentary attendance to Chamber events.
  • Dedicated support from a Chamber liaison.
  • Priority invitations to exclusive stakeholder roundtables.
  • Opportunities for strategic partnerships and collaborations.
